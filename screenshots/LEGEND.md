# FitzFace Feature Legend

## Numbered Features (see annotated_numbered.png)

1. **City Name (GPS)** - Automatically detected location via reverse geocoding
2. **Today Weather Icon** - Current weather conditions
3. **Tomorrow Weather Icon** - Tomorrow's weather forecast
4. **Date** - Current date (day of week, month, day)
5. **Time** - Large digital time display
6. **Pollen Level** - Tree/grass/weed pollen index (T/G/W + level)
7. **Weather Alert** - Smart alerts for weather and health conditions
8. **MUNI Bus Countdown** - Next SF bus arrivals in minutes (requires 511.org API key)
9. **Current Temperature** - Real-time temperature
10. **High | Low Temps** - Today's temperature range
11. **Wind Speed** - Current wind conditions
12. **UV Index** - Ultraviolet radiation level
13. **Air Quality (AQI)** - US Air Quality Index
14. **Next Tide** - Upcoming tide time and type (H/L)
15. **Sunrise Time** - Morning sunrise
16. **Sunset Time** - Evening sunset

---

**Data Sources**: Open-Meteo (Weather/AQI), NOAA (Tides), 511.org (MUNI), Google (Pollen), OpenStreetMap (Location)
